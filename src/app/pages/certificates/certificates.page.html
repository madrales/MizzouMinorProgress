MIZZOU COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING UNDERGRADUATE CERTIFICATES

On this page, you will find a comprehensive list of the undergraduate certificates offered by the College of Engineering at Mizzou.

Information provided by Mizzou College of Engineering
CYBERSECURITY CERTIFICATE 15 This stand-alone certificate is offered online and is highly focused on real-world use cases through multiple hands-on laboratories and up-to-date training.
INFORMATION SYSTEMS CERTIFICATE 12 This 12-credit hour stand-alone certificate is focused on technology used in most business workplaces. You’ll get a survey-level introduction to practices, design, programming and web application development.
WEB & MEDIA APP DEVELOPMENT CERTIFICATE 12 This stand-alone certificate is focused on web application and mobile application development. You will learn various programming languages, front- and back-end design and database management for both iOS and Android.
MEDIA TECHNOLOGY & DESIGN CERTIFICATE 12 This stand-alone certificate focuses on post-production, editorial, animation and media technology skills. Learn non-linear editing, 2D and 3D animation, motion graphics, visual effects, audio post-production, basic game design principles and production system designs.
